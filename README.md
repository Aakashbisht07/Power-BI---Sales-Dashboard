# Power-BI - Sales-Dashboard
INTRODUCTION:

This Sales Dashboard is designed to provide a comprehensive and interactive overview of business performance across different regions, product categories, and time periods. The dashboard analyzes key metrics such as Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Discount to help stakeholders understand overall trends and identify areas of growth and improvement.
Using interactive visuals, slicers, and filters, users can explore sales performance by region, state, city, category, sub-category, and year. The dashboard enables quick comparison of sales and profit across regions, identification of top-performing and low-performing products, and evaluation of the impact of discounts on profitability.
The primary objective of this dashboard is to support data-driven decision-making by transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights. It helps business users monitor performance, detect patterns, and make informed strategic decisions to improve revenue and operational efficiency.
